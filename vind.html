VINDINIUM

Four legendary heroes were fighting for the land of Vindinium
Making their way in the dangerous woods
Slashing goblins and stealing gold mines
And looking for a tavern where to drink their gold

So, what is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a game up to four players battle it out to take mines and end up with the most amount of gold. They can go to taverns to heal, attack other players, or most importantly, capture mines. The catch is in how you control the bots. Rather than directly controlling them, you must program an AI in any programming language you prefer. The intelligence of these bots varies from the most basic level of taking the most direct path to a mine regardless of the obstacles, or to using complex pathing systems that calculate the cost and risk of moving to an area.
Everyone also has 100 health, loses 20 health from players and goblins, and can heal for 50 health at taverns. Additionally, if you die, you lose all your mines to your killer, but keep any gold you've accumulated. Mines are the source of gold, giving out 1 gold per mine every turn.
